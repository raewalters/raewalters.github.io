Off Campus Library Services (OCLS) serves the students, faculty, and staff of the National and Gloabl Campus (N&G).

Most adult learners take their courses online or at education centers, and these students should contact OCLS when assistance is needed.

‍

‍

OCLS provide IWU National & Global students and faculty with the resources needed for developing character, scholarship, and leadership.

‍

OCLS faculty librarians provide support and assistance with research, resources, library technology, APA Style, Chicago Manual Style, classroom instruction, and webinars.

‍

OCLS supports students with resources, instruction, and tools to help them succeed and SHINE BRIGHTER.
